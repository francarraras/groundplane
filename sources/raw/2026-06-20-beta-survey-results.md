# Beta Survey Results — Round One

Collected 2026-06-14 through 2026-06-19, summarized 2026-06-20. 21 of 25 testers responded.

## Habit

- 14 of 21 journal in the morning; morning journalers logged 5+ entries/week.
- Evening journalers averaged 2.7 entries/week and cited forgetting as the reason.

## Sentiment

- "Would you miss Skylark if it disappeared?" — 17 yes, 3 maybe, 1 no.
- Net promoter style score: 41.

## Pricing

- 16 of 21 prefer a one-time purchase; median acceptable price 5–7 USD.

## Top requests (not launch blockers)

1. Month export (7 mentions)
2. Reminder timing options (6 mentions)
3. Rain-only alert mode (4 mentions)

## Action

Default onboarding to a morning reminder; queue export and reminder-timing for the v1.1 scope draft (TASK-080). Survey round two (TASK-077) waits on the survey-tooling fix.
