# Skylark Pricing Decision Worksheet

Worked 2026-06-16 by Mara and Theo. Feeds decision DEC-001.

## Options considered

1. One-time purchase, 6 USD. Simple, honest, matches a journal you own. No recurring revenue.
2. Subscription, 1.50 USD/month. Steadier revenue, but testers reacted badly to renting a journal.
3. Freemium plus tip jar. Widest reach, weakest signal, most support surface.

## Signals

- Beta survey: 16 of 21 respondents preferred paying once; median acceptable price 5–7 USD.
- Support load projection favors one SKU with no entitlement edge cases at launch.
- Store checklist is simpler with a single paid listing.

## Recommendation

Lock one-time purchase at 6 USD for Skylark 1.0. Revisit only after the launch-week retro (TASK-080). Any change to price is a review-packet decision, never a quiet edit.
