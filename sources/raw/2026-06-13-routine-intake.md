# Routine Intake Capture

Messy bullets pasted by the studio pair on 2026-06-13. Basis for the launch-week routines.

- Launch standup - every morning - 15 minutes - current gate, blockers, one decision max
- Beta feedback triage - daily after standup - label new playtest notes and tickets, escalate anything crashy
- Support digest - weekly - compile ticket themes into one digest note for the catalog
- Ship review - weekly - look at what actually shipped, update proof packages, prune stale tasks
- Books and invoices - monthly - close the month, file receipts - keep parked during launch crunch
- What derails us: untriaged feedback piling up, and silent scope creep on the website
- The assistant should enforce: one gate at a time, and no durable writes without a review packet
