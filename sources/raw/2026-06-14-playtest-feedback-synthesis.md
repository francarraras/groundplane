# Playtest Feedback Synthesis — Round One

Synthesized 2026-06-14 from 25 beta playtest sessions of Skylark (weather-journal app).

## What worked

- The journal editor felt fast; nobody lost an entry.
- The daily forecast strip was read correctly by 23 of 25 testers.
- Morning testers journaled the most consistently; evening testers skipped days.

## Findings

- C001: first-run permission prompt appeared twice on one device profile. Fixed in beta 3 during the session week; no packet needed.
- C002: nine testers read the units toggle (°C/°F) as a theme switch and tapped it expecting dark mode. Label and icon reworked in beta 4; confusion stopped in follow-up sessions.
- C003: three testers asked for a way to export a month of entries; logged as a v1.1 candidate, not a launch blocker.

## Signals for launch

- Onboarding should default to a morning reminder; that is when the habit sticks.
- Nothing in round one blocks the store submission gate.
